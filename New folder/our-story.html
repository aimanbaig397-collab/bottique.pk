Our Story

Andreas Boutiques brings together bridal and occasion wear with the finest lace, silk, and craftsmanship.

Visit us for a personal fitting or browse our collections online.

Our Journey

  • 2010

    Andreas Boutiques opened its first atelier with a focus on bespoke bridal wear.

  • 2015

    Expanded into evening wear and launched our first ready-to-wear collection.

  • 2018

    Moved to our current flagship store and introduced personal fitting appointments.

  • 2022

    Launched online boutique and nationwide shipping for select collections.

  • 2025

    Today we continue to craft timeless pieces for weddings and special occasions.
